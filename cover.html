Application for NRL Sports Scientist, Gold Coast Titans

Dear Titans Performance Team,

I am applying for the NRL Sports Scientist role. I am currently an NRL Sports Scientist at the Penrith Panthers, so I know the rhythm of the competition first hand: the weekly reporting cadence, the game-day demands, and the constant work of turning monitoring data into decisions that performance, medical and coaching staff actually use.

My day-to-day is built on the systems you use. I operate Catapult and VALD, run conditioning testing and monitoring, and produce the weekly reporting suite that covers GPS outputs, wellness, screening, body mass and match performance. I have managed return-to-play across rugby league, AFL and basketball, coordinating with medical staff to progress athletes through clear, objective milestones, and I am comfortable owning data quality across a performance department.

Where I think I add something extra is automation. I build the reporting pipelines and dashboards myself in R and Python, and I increasingly use AI tooling such as Claude Code to streamline the work, automating the parts of reporting that usually eat an analyst's week so staff get cleaner insight, faster. I noticed the role calls this out as highly desirable; it is already how I work, and I keep a portfolio of deidentified dashboards and workflows I am happy to walk through.

The Titans values of being selfless, trustworthy, accountable and relentless line up with how I operate. I value the person before the athlete, I keep athletes on the field by addressing issues early, and I hold myself accountable for the quality and clarity of everything I put in front of a coach. I would relish the chance to bring that to the Gold Coast.

Thank you for considering my application. I would love to discuss how I can contribute to the club's performance program.
