Portfolio

Agentic Workflows & Dashboards

A selection of systems I have designed and built end to end, from data pipeline to interface. These power real operations in professional sport, e-commerce and marketing. The diagrams and mockups below are representative; client and athlete data has been removed.

Every example here is deidentified. Client and club names, athlete data and commercially sensitive figures have been replaced with representative content. Live walkthroughs of the real systems are available on request.

Professional sport

Athlete monitoring and live game dashboards

Built with R, Shiny, SQLite, Catapult and live tracking APIs, automated HTML report generation

A suite of dashboards for an elite rugby league program. A GPS and athlete-demand dashboard turns Catapult data into session loads, readiness and individual deep-dives, while a live in-game tool tracks ball-in-play, interchange and player minutes from real-time feeds. Pre and post-game reports are generated automatically as shareable pages for performance, medical and coaching staff.

Representative squad readiness and weekly load view. Real dashboards integrate GPS, force plate, wellness and screening data.

E-commerce operations

Live Ops platform with agentic alerting

Built with Next.js, FastAPI, Postgres and Supabase, Shopify and 3PL fulfilment integrations, Claude for narrative generation, Railway

An operational monitoring platform for a direct-to-consumer eyewear brand. It unifies orders, fulfilment, stock forecast and tracking into one Live Ops view. An agentic layer watches the signals, raises plain-language flags, and posts a written briefing to Slack so the team catches problems before they cascade.

Order, fulfilment and stock signals flow into a flags engine that writes a plain-language briefing to Slack each morning.

Marketing, Subject:RAW

Growth OS demand-report engine

Built with Python, FastAPI, Postgres, Jinja and HTMX, search, places and ad-library data, Claude, Railway

A white-label engine that turns a clinic's website, location and segment into a credible one-page market report showing the local patient demand they are missing. It runs a pipeline from intake to data pull to demand calculation to assembly and quality check, then exports a branded report as both web page and PDF. Includes an SEO and visibility audit for the same clients.

A website and postcode become a one-page report. The QA step applies claim guardrails before anything ships.

Marketing, Subject:RAW

Automated creative and graphics workflow

Built with Custom app, brand-token system, templated render pipeline

A custom application that automates repeatable design and graphics production for an agency. A brief and a brand token set drive an automated render step; a human reviews, and approved variants flow straight to output. It standardises brand output across clients and lets a small team produce a far larger volume of consistent work.

Briefs become on-brand assets through a templated render step, with a human review loop before output.

Finance and retail

Sales-deck generator

Built with Python, FastAPI, python-pptx, LibreOffice headless, Claude, Railway

A tool that converts raw market notes into a real, editable PowerPoint deck. It extracts a brand kit from a template, builds native editable charts rather than flat images, and supports slide-by-slide refinement through plain-English feedback, with live preview and PDF export.

Notes become a real editable deck with native charts, refined slide by slide in plain English.
